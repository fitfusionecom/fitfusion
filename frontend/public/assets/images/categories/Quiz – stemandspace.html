Frequently Asked Questions

What age group is the quiz suitable for?

The quiz is designed for children aged 8 to 14 years who have an interest in space and astronomy.

How does the quiz work?

Our quiz is designed to be fun and engaging for kids interested in space and astronomy. Participants will answer 20 questions related to space exploration, careers, and astronomy. Upon completion, they will receive a certificate and an opportunity to claim a free illustrated comic on space careers.

How do I participate in the quiz?

To participate, simply visit our website and navigate to the quiz section. Follow the instructions to begin answering the questions. It's that easy!

Is there a cost to participate in the quiz?

No, participating in the quiz is entirely free! We are committed to providing access to space education for all curious children. However, participants are required to cover the shipping charges, which amount to INR 90+ GST.

Can parents participate in the quiz with their children?

While the quiz is primarily aimed at children, parents are welcome to participate and enjoy the experience together with their kids.

What if I have more questions or need assistance?

If you have any additional questions or need assistance, please feel free to reach out to our dedicated support team at events@stemandspace.com. We are here to help!
